Adventure Awaits

Embark on a captivating journey through Chattanooga's favorite sights, where adventure and charm converge. Start your exploration at the renowned Tennessee Aquarium, a world-class destination showcasing the wonders of the deep. Immerse yourself in the Ocean and River Journey exhibits, encountering fascinating marine life and gaining insight into conservation efforts. For sports enthusiasts, the Chattanooga Lookouts offer an electric experience at AT&T Field, where the crack of the bat and the cheers of the crowd create lasting memories. Meanwhile, Coolidge Park beckons with its scenic beauty and family-friendly ambiance. From the antique carousel to the historic Walnut Street Bridge, the park provides a perfect blend of relaxation and excitement, making it a must-visit destination for locals and visitors alike. In Chattanooga, every sight tells a unique story, promising a delightful and diverse experience for all who venture into this captivating city.

Tennessee Aquarium - Dive into the Wonders of the Deep

Welcome to the Tennessee Aquarium, where the mysteries of the aquatic world come to life. Nestled in the heart of Chattanooga, our world-class facility invites you on an unforgettable journey through rivers, oceans, and beyond.

Ocean Journey - Immerse yourself in the wonders of the ocean as you journey through our spectacular exhibits. From playful penguins to mesmerizing jellyfish, every corner unveils a new underwater spectacle.
River Journey - Discover the diverse ecosystems of the Tennessee River and its tributaries. Encounter fascinating creatures, including turtles, otters, and an array of freshwater fish.

Coolidge Park - Where Fun Meets Scenic Beauty

Welcome to Coolidge Park, a picturesque oasis nestled on the banks of the Tennessee River. With a perfect blend of green spaces, family-friendly activities, and stunning views, Coolidge Park is a must-visit destination for locals and tourists alike.

Take a ride on the antique carousel, a nostalgic centerpiece for visitors of all ages. The adjoining pavilion offers a shaded retreat with a view of the river. Cool off in the interactive fountains, a perfect way for kids to splash and play on warm days.

Stroll across the historic Walnut Street Bridge, one of the world's longest pedestrian bridges. Enjoy panoramic views of the river and downtown Chattanooga.

Chattanooga Lookouts - Home Runs and Family Fun

Welcome to the home of the Chattanooga Lookouts, where the crack of the bat and the cheers of the crowd create an electric atmosphere. Join us for an unforgettable experience of America's favorite pastime right here in Chattanooga.

Cheer on the Lookouts at AT&T Field, a classic ballpark with modern amenities. Immerse yourself in the thrill of minor league baseball in a family-friendly environment.

Check our schedule for exciting promotions and events throughout the season. From fireworks nights to themed giveaways, there's always something special happening at the ballpark.
