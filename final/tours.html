Chattanooga Duck Boat Tours - Navigate the Scenic City by Land and Water

Embark on a unique adventure with Chattanooga Duck Boat Tours, where sightseeing takes on a whole new dimension. These amphibious vehicles let you explore the city's iconic landmarks by both land and water. Cruise along the Tennessee River and then seamlessly transition onto the streets for a guided tour of Chattanooga's historic sites. With entertaining narrators, fun facts, and breathtaking views, this tour offers a refreshing and immersive way to experience the scenic beauty and rich history of the city.

Ruby Falls - Unearth the Wonders of the Underground World

Descend into the depths of Lookout Mountain and discover the mesmerizing beauty of Ruby Falls, an underground waterfall and natural wonder. Guided cave tours take you through a network of stunning rock formations, leading to the breathtaking cascade of Ruby Falls itself. Illuminated by carefully placed lights, the caverns create a magical, otherworldly atmosphere. Explore the geological marvels and learn about the fascinating history of Ruby Falls, making it a must-visit destination for nature enthusiasts and curious adventurers.

Chattanooga Whiskey's Experimental Distillery - A Spirited Journey into Craftsmanship

Immerse yourself in the world of craft spirits at Chattanooga Whiskey's Experimental Distillery. This unique tour offers an insider's look into the art and science of whiskey making. Explore the distillation process, from grain to glass, and witness the innovative techniques used in crafting small-batch, handcrafted spirits. Engage with knowledgeable guides, sample a selection of their finest creations, and gain a newfound appreciation for the craftsmanship behind each bottle. Chattanooga Whiskey's Experimental Distillery promises a spirited journey for whiskey connoisseurs and those eager to explore the nuances of artisanal distillation.
